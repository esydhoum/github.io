About DEWLA SEAFOOD

Our Story

DEWLA SEAFOOD is a trusted name in the seafood industry, proudly specializing in the production, processing, and export of premium frozen seafood products sourced from the rich and pristine waters of the Atlantic coast of Mauritania.

With a deep commitment to quality, sustainability, and freshness, DEWLA SEAFOOD ensures that every product meets the highest international standards — from the moment it is caught to the time it reaches our customers worldwide. Our modern facilities are equipped with advanced freezing and packaging technologies that preserve the natural taste, texture, and nutritional value of the seafood.

At DEWLA SEAFOOD, we work closely with local fishermen and use responsible fishing methods to protect marine life and promote the long-term sustainability of ocean resources. Our diverse range of products includes a wide selection of fish, cephalopods, and crustaceans, carefully processed and packed to satisfy the needs of global markets.

Driven by passion and professionalism, our team strives to deliver excellence in every shipment — ensuring that our partners and clients always receive seafood that is fresh, flavorful, and full of the Atlantic's authentic taste.

Whether supplying wholesalers, retailers, or food service companies, DEWLA SEAFOOD continues to build a reputation as one of Mauritania's leading seafood exporters, connecting the ocean's bounty with tables around the world.
